About the Video "Viva La Vida"

The song "Viva La Vida" was released in 2008 as part of the album Viva la Vida or Death and All His Friends. The music video features artistic visuals inspired by historical and revolutionary themes. The title translates to "Live the Life" in Spanish, and the song became one of Coldplay’s biggest hits worldwide.

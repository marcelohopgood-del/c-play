Who Coldplay Is

Coldplay is a British rock band formed in 1997. The group consists of Chris Martin (lead vocals and piano), Jonny Buckland (guitar), Guy Berryman (bass), and Will Champion (drums). They are known for their emotional lyrics, powerful melodies, and global success in the alternative rock and pop rock genres.
